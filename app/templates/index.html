What’s to come

Set Talents is a site to revolutionise the entertainment industry. The focus of this web application and soon to be mobile app is to simplify industry agencies and talent management. Basically giving a transparent and fluid contact platform for agencies, talents and clients to send and receive inquires.

Agencies

Agencies will be able to have their agency be seen locally and internationally. This will allow an easier process of sending models abroad and having you agency visible to international agencies to send models to your location.

Talents

Models will be able to have their portfolio, snaps and reel all under their profile making it their responsibility to keep it up to date and quicker for agencies to view.

Clients

Clients will have the ability to register for the app and have full access to the model roster of who is in their local area and the agency they are with to directly request. Clients will also have the ability to send inquires for castings and jobs out directly to agencies, allowing agencies to directly forward it to their models.
